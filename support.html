Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a card?

Navigate to the Scan tab, point your camera at an MTG card, and hold steady. The app uses on-device OCR to identify the card name. Make sure the card name is clearly visible and well-lit for best results.

How does cloud sync work?

Sign in with Apple to enable cloud sync. Your collection, binders, and decks will automatically sync across all your Apple devices. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

How are card prices calculated?

Card prices are sourced in real time from Scryfall, which aggregates pricing data from major card marketplaces. Prices are updated each time you view a card or refresh your collection.

What's included in Silvan Library Pro?

Pro unlocks unlimited binders and decks, full collection analytics with price tracking, AI-powered deck coaching, cloud sync across devices, set completion tracking, and data export. Free users can create up to 3 binders and 3 decks.

How do I import a deck?

Go to the Decks tab, tap the import button, and choose from Moxfield URL, Archidekt URL, or paste a decklist in standard text format (one card per line, quantity followed by card name).

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and select Silvan Library to manage or cancel your subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings (Profile tab), and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently remove all your cloud-synced data. Local data on your device will also be cleared.

The card scanner isn't recognizing my card

Make sure the card is well-lit with the name clearly visible. Hold your phone steady about 6-8 inches from the card. Foil cards and older sets with unusual fonts may take a moment longer. The scanner works best with a dark background behind the card.

System Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • iPhone with camera (for card scanning)
  • Internet connection (for card prices and cloud sync)
